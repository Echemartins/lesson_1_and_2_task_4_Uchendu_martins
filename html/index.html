St Anthonys

The purpose for which this corporation was formed is to encourage and foster a social and civil spirit and to promote and propagate fraternal principles among its members and their families, in case of death or distress.and to raise funds through special events for distribution to the Church where the celebration of the Feast is held, other Churches in the area, Seminaries and to aid families and individuals in need.

Sacred Heart

We answer God's call - to discover and make known the love of the Heart of Jesus..We are an order of women in the Catholic Church. We are called to be contemplative in spirit, generous and educative in mission, and passionate for justice, peace, and the integrity of creation.

Mary League

The primary mission of this Association is to pray and promote Marian devotions and the Rosary. The Association prays the Rosary before every daily mass and Every 1st Saturday Monthly Meeting. Morning Prayer and novena are offered. Commitment: as determined by Association.Together we are learning to live life the way Mary did: knowing the Mother loves us, empowered by the Holy Spirit, caring for each other, celebrating creativity, being adventurous in our faith, reaching out to those around, living honestly and devoted to God our Father.

The Charistmatic Renewal

Catholic Charismatic Renewal (CCR) is a spiritual movement within the Catholic Church that emphasises the availability of the power and the many gifts of the Holy Spirit in the life of every believer, and the need for a personal relationship with God through Jesus Christ in order to live life to the fullest. The Precious Blood

Devotion to the Precious Blood of Jesus is one of the oldest devotions in the Church. Our Blessed Mother adored the Most Precious Blood of her infant Son on the day of His circumcision as the first drops of His Precious Blood were shed amongst many tears for the salvation of our sinful humanity.
